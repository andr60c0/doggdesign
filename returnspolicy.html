Return Policy

Send a message to us!

  • Email Dögg Design at dogg@doggdesign.com.

  • Subject title: Return ordered goods.

  • Wait for reply from Dögg Design.

  • Make sure the product is securely packed.

  • Attach a copy of order confirmation. Note if you want the product refunded or exchanged. If exchanged note to what product.

Note: We do not accept packed sent COD. You can also return products by denying it upon receipt or by delivering personally to our showroom at

Prøvestensbroen 3, 2300 Copenhagen S.

If you deny receipt upon delivery we will charge you a handling fee.

For questions and concerns please email dogg@doggdesign.com.
